About Us

University of Nebraska at Omaha

This project was built by Vivek Bhat Hosmat, a graduate student at University of Nebraska Omaha under the mentorship of Dr. Sachin Pawaskar. This web application was a product of the proposal made to Office of Research and Creativity for the Graduate Research and Creative Activity (GRACA) award.

Our aim was to showcase the use of Exploratory Visual analytics in understanding the data set before diving deep to answer specific research/analysis questions

We used varied tools such D3.JS- a javascript library for data visualization and a more simplistic Google Data studio for our visualizations
